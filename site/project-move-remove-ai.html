Move & Remove AI — Computer Vision Quoting

An AI-powered quoting concept that turns photos into structured estimates in minutes.

Timeframe: Placeholder

Context

Quoting relies on photo context, and delays reduce conversion. A faster workflow improves customer trust.

Problem

Manual quote review is time-consuming and inconsistent when teams are busy.

Goals & Success Metrics

Placeholders: faster quote turnaround, consistent pricing, higher close rates.

Constraints & Tradeoffs

  • Edge cases with mixed or unclear photos
  • Human-in-the-loop review required for accuracy
  • Clear customer expectations for AI usage

Approach

Designed a photo submission flow, structured extraction, and a review queue for final approval.

Solution

  • Mobile-first photo upload with prompts
  • AI extraction for load size and item types
  • Reviewer checklist for final pricing
  • Automated quote delivery and follow-up

Impact

Placeholders: 30% faster quotes and more consistent estimates.

What I’d Do Next

Run a pilot with limited categories to validate accuracy before scaling.

Learnings

  • AI workflows need clear human escalation paths
  • Prompted photo intake improves data quality
  • Speed creates trust in service businesses
