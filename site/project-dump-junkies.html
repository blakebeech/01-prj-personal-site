Dump Junkies CLT — CRM & Operations Automation

A complete lead-to-job workflow that improved consistency across quoting, scheduling, and coordination.

Role: Founder / Product Builder
Timeframe: Placeholder
Team: Solo with ops input
Tools: Go High Level, pipelines, forms, automations
Back to Projects

Context

Dump Junkies CLT is a service business with real-time scheduling and field operations. Lead flow needed a reliable, fast system.

Problem

Manual handoffs caused delays, missed follow-ups, and inconsistent quotes.

Goals & Success Metrics

Placeholders: faster lead response, higher close rate, fewer scheduling errors.

Constraints & Tradeoffs

  • Minimal training time for crews
  • Automation must not break when workload spikes
  • CRM data quality needed to stay high

Approach

Mapped the full workflow, identified key handoffs, and designed a single pipeline from intake to completion.

Solution

  • Standardized lead capture forms with validation
  • Automated quote and follow-up sequences
  • Scheduling view for field coordination
  • Status-based alerts for stalled jobs

Impact

Placeholders: improved lead-to-job conversion, reduced admin hours, more predictable scheduling.

What I’d Do Next

Expand analytics dashboards and integrate customer feedback loops.

Learnings

  • Operational clarity beats complex tooling
  • Automation must align with real-world constraints
  • Stakeholder buy-in reduces downstream friction
