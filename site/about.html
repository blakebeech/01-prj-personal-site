My Product Story

A product-minded builder currently finishing my senior year at the University of South Carolina, while owning my own field service company. My path into product hasn't been traditional: I learned by doing, automating my own operations, supporting innovation research and market scans in healthcare, and building growth tooling for other firms and personal ventures.

What drives me is the intersection of technology and real-world operations. I'm fascinated by how thoughtful AI and automation can free people to do more meaningful work, and how user-centered design can transform frustrating experiences into delightful ones. Whether I'm optimizing scheduling flows for my crew or researching AI applications in business, I bring the same approach: understand the problem deeply, prototype quickly, then review and revise based on real feedback until it is time to ship.

Outside of work, you'll find me exploring Charlotte's food scene, playing pickup basketball, or diving into the latest AI release or product podcasts. I believe the best PMs are perpetual students, curious about everything and humble enough to know there's always more to learn.
